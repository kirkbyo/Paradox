Process

  1. Initial Meeting: We will discuss your project and what your vision is for it.

  2. Start to Design: We will begin the inital steps of the design process and will start creating serval concepts.

  3. Finalize Design: We will finalize the design, in order for the real magic to start happening. This isn't 100% final there most likely will be a lot of tweaks and changes, but it's the final concept.

  4. Start of production: The fun part begins. We will start creating your project.

  5. First Beta Test: You will get to use your project for the first time and can voice your opinion on how it feels and looks. (25% Built)

  6. Second Beta Test: The project is starting to look more like your vision.(50% Built)

  7. Third Beta Test: Your project is almost complete and can really start using it.(95% Built)

  8. Final Beta Test: We apply any changes you want implemented and gather your final feed back. (100% Built)

  9. Launch: We help you release the your project into the real world.
