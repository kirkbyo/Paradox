Outflow

Subscription Manager

Outflow was created because of how hard it was to keep track of all your subscriptions. The app was created with the main idea to have a simple easy place to manage all your growing number of subscriptions.

Created over a period of six months and was our first big project. Over that period of time, we overcame several design and engineering decisions that almost prevented us from creating the app you see.

  • Time Tracking

    Keep track of how many days until it renews.

  • View All

    Quickly view all your subscriptions

  • Notifications

    Choose to be alerted the day of renewal or week before.

  • Cash Flow

    Keep track of when a company is renewing.
